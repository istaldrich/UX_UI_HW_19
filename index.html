About Me

My name is Isabelle. I am a UX/UI designer based in Minneapolis, Minnesota. I am pragmatic, curious, and creative.

My favorite thing about design is its ability to influence an individual’s experience and path through a digital space. I believe that empathizing with users is the root to creating an effective, cohesive, and inviting interface design.

I have leveraged design principles to create mobile apps, websites, and responsive website redesigns. I have also observed, researched, and experimented with methods to effectively implement aspects of design to bring my visions to life.

I am eager to develop my skills as a UX/UI designer and create digital spaces that individuals find captivating and enjoyable to use.
